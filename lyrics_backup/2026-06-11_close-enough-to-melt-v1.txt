[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The porch light flickers when you lean in slow
A heavy breath and the shadows grow
My wandering heart finally meets with yours
We leave the world outside these doors

[Verse 1]
The midnight moon on your skin looks right
You pull me deep in the calm of the night
A quiet spark in a darkened room
We trace the lines where the embers bloom

[Pre-Chorus]
Every step you take is a pull on my soul
I lose my grip and I let go of control
The closer we get, the deeper I fall
Just give me your touch, I am risking it all

[Chorus]
We are close enough to melt tonight
Burning up in this candlelight
Got your hands on me and my heart on fire
Slipping away in a sweet desire

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, close enough to melt into you
[Echo] (Melt into you, melt into you)

[Verse 2]
